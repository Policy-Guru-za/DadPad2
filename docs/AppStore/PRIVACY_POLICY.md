# PolishPad Privacy Policy

Effective date: 24 April 2026

PolishPad is a private iPad writing utility that helps users turn rough typed or dictated text into clearer notes, emails, and messages using on-device Apple Intelligence.

This Privacy Policy explains how PolishPad handles information.

## Summary

PolishPad is designed to work without an account, without advertising, and without collecting your writing.

We do not collect, store, sell, or share the text you type, dictate, polish, copy, or share in the app.

## Information We Collect

PolishPad does not collect personal information from users.

The app does not require you to create an account. The app does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, health information, or payment information.

## Text You Enter Into the App

Text that you type or dictate into PolishPad is used only to provide the app’s writing features on your device.

PolishPad does not send your draft text or polished output to our servers.

PolishPad does not store your draft text or polished output on a server.

PolishPad does not sell or share your draft text or polished output.

## On-Device Processing

PolishPad is designed to process text on device using Apple Intelligence on compatible iPads.

If Apple Intelligence is not available, enabled, downloaded, language-supported, and ready on your iPad, PolishPad does not send your text to a cloud service or provide a lower-quality fallback rewrite.

## Dictation

PolishPad relies on the standard iPad keyboard and iPadOS dictation features. If you choose to dictate text, that dictation experience is provided by Apple’s system keyboard and iPadOS.

PolishPad does not provide a custom speech-recognition service and does not record or upload audio.

## Copying and Sharing

If you choose to copy text, the copied text is placed on the iPadOS clipboard.

If you choose to share text, sharing is handled through Apple’s standard system sharing interface. You control which app or service receives the shared text.

Once you copy or share text outside PolishPad, the handling of that text is governed by the app, service, or recipient you choose.

## Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

PolishPad does not use third-party analytics SDKs.

PolishPad does not show advertising.

PolishPad does not track you across apps or websites.

PolishPad does not use third-party tracking tools.

## Children’s Privacy

PolishPad is a general writing utility and is not designed to collect information from children.

Because PolishPad does not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Retention

Because PolishPad does not collect your personal information or writing on our servers, we do not retain that information.

Text may remain visible in the app while you are using it, and may remain available on your device according to normal iPadOS behaviour. You can clear text inside the app using the Clear action.

## Security

PolishPad is designed to minimise privacy risk by avoiding accounts, server-side storage, advertising, analytics, and cloud text processing.

No system can be guaranteed completely secure, but PolishPad’s design intentionally limits the information available to us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
