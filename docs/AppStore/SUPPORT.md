# PolishPad Support

PolishPad is a private iPad writing utility that helps users turn rough typed or dictated text into clearer notes, emails, and messages.

## Contact Support

For support, contact:

support@redcliffebay.com

Please include:
- your iPad model, if known
- your iPadOS version
- a short description of the issue
- the steps that caused the issue

Please do not send private or sensitive text from the app unless it is necessary for support.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an account?

No. PolishPad does not require an account.

### Does PolishPad send my text to a server?

No. PolishPad is designed to process text on device where available and does not send your draft or polished text to our servers.

### How do I dictate text?

Tap into the writing area, then use the standard iPad keyboard dictation microphone.

### Why is polishing unavailable on my iPad?

Some polishing features depend on Apple system capabilities and device availability. If those features are unavailable, PolishPad may provide a basic local fallback.

### Can I copy or share the result?

Yes. You can copy text to the clipboard or share it using the standard iPadOS share sheet.

### Is PolishPad a medical or therapy app?

No. PolishPad is a writing utility. It is not a medical, clinical, diagnostic, or therapy app.
